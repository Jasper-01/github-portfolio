Dankook University
My 4 years of Bachelor's Degree in Global Software.
Background
I spent the year 2020 working in between lockdown due to the COVID-19, which delayed my decision to enroll into Dankook University from 2020 to spring of 2021.
The reason I chose Dankook University was because I attended one of their Edutainment trips hosted in collaboration with Study Hub Asia back in December 2019, where the technology and culture of the country awed me in many ways.
Academic
Initially, I was enrolled into Mobile Systems Engineering. However, due to some issues regarding certain classes, some classes that I needed to attend were unavailable and would hinder my graduation. Thus, under the advice of the head of department professor at that time, I switched to Global Software which would provide similar classes.
Thanks to my professors who have been patient and giving their best, I have continued to polish my skills and futher my understanding in many fields.
University Life
As a foreigner
As a foreigner with the absolute minimal ability to converse in Korean when I first arrived, it was difficult to navigate in finding friends. Fortunately, I found like-minded friends in my course and also other foreigners in Korea, especially International students. I was lucky to be acquianted with many Global Talent Network (a.k.a GTN) members, which allowed me to find my footing in a new country. In my first semester, I was assigned a buddy, Jiny, who was good in English and a very sweet person.
Taekwondo Club
Though it was only for a semester, I was able to take time out of my busy schedule to join the taekwondo club in the university. Thanks to my seniors, I was able to gain confidence in speaking Korean.
Traveling
Occasionally, I would go traveling with friends to experience different parts of the country, including Han River, Gangneung, Hongdae, and etc.
